About

Student nightlife experience, without overcomplicating it.

I’m a University of Kent student DJ based in Kent, with experience playing clubs, bars, student society events, sports club socials, formal events and university promotions across Canterbury.

My strength is adapting to the room. I can move between commercial chart music, 2000s/2010s throwbacks, pop, hip-hop, dance classics, sing-alongs and well-known reggaeton depending on the crowd and event.
